What We Do

Our Services

Whether you need a single truckload or an ongoing dedicated solution, CH Logistics has the capacity, cross-border expertise, and commitment to keep your supply chain moving.

Full Truckload (FTL)

Dedicated trailers for large shipments moving across Canada or into the U.S. Maximum efficiency for high-volume freight with direct point-to-point delivery.

Less-Than-Truckload (LTL)

Cost-effective shipping for smaller loads. Share trailer space while benefiting from our extensive network and reliable scheduling across North America.

Cross-Border Transport

Seamless Canada–USA freight movement with full customs compliance, border expertise, and documentation to keep your shipments flowing without delays.

Dedicated Fleet

Consistent, contracted capacity with drivers who know your lanes. Ideal for businesses with regular, predictable shipping needs requiring above-all reliability.

Expedited Freight

Time-critical shipments demand a higher response level. Priority handling and accelerated delivery guaranteed across the continent.

Temperature-Controlled

Refrigerated and climate-controlled transport for perishables, pharmaceuticals, and sensitive goods. Full cold-chain integrity from pickup to delivery.

About CH Logistics

Built on
Reliability

CH Logistics is a Burlington, Ontario-based carrier with over 15 years of experience moving freight across Canada and the United States. We started with a simple commitment: show up on time, every time.

Strategically located near the QEW and major border crossings, we serve clients in manufacturing, retail, food & beverage, and industrial sectors. Our drivers, dispatchers, and operations team work around the clock to keep your supply chain moving.
